Artikraft is our trademark for our product line. Arti derives from a Sanskrit word which means "meaning", while Kraft derives from English word "craft" which means "handmade"

In every piece of "Artikraft" product, there is a meaning that defines the product's purpose, and there is a story that conveys the artisanal workmanship going into each product.

Environmental Social Responsibilty

We use mainly fast growing natural fiber as our raw materials. We manufacture our products in eco-friendly and efficient manner. keep up with the increasing demand for world class quality products

The industry's high standard challanges us to balance business expansion with social and environmental responsibility

Our Vision

Our vision is to use eco-friendly materials, and to manufacture exclusive quality handicrafts and home furnishings. We strive to sustain our natural resources, empower our surrounding communities, and become a leader in the global marketplace

Our Value

We believe in using HIGHLY RENEWABLE raw materials to maintain SUSTAINABILITY. We are all about protecting our natural resources from damage and destruction. We strive to strike a balance between what we take from earth and what we give back to the earth.

We create opportunities for local artisans to earn income by working and growing together through long-term and ETHICALLY RESPONSIBLE relationship. We believe in partnering with local communities to upgrade their standards of living and to DEVELOP THEIR HUMAN CAPITAL

We believe in applying EFFICIENT ECO-FRIENDLY production method to manufacture excellent quality product. All of our products are produced using all-natural process to ensure they are SAFE and suitable for use, and they do not harm our environtment.
